=== COMMENT 13 ===

It's a very conceptual class. Probably depends who teaches it, but Dr. Lynch was quite hard. I'd recommend doing all the reading before classes. Even if you won't understand everything (the textbook can be hard to read) at least you'll know what you should pay attention to during the lecture. And start your homeworks early. You can get a lot of really good advice if you come to office hours. You don't really have to prepare for the class in general. The concepts are explained from lvl 0. But they can get hard pretty quick, so just pay attention to this class. Plus, a little bit of Python or Java programming skills are needed (but really quite basic stuff). You'll notice that you'll get lost in the class pretty often, but a lot of people did when I took the class, so don't worry.
=== END COMMENT 13 ===
